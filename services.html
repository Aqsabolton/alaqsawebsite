Our Religious & Social Services

01 • Education

Evening Madrasah

Providing structured spiritual upbringing, Quranic recitation (Tajweed), and foundational Islamic ethics for the youth of Bolton. Nurturing the next generation with balanced sacred wisdom.

🕒 Early Class: Mon - Fri | 4:40 pm – 5:55 pm
🕒 Late Class: Mon - Fri | 6:05 pm – 7:20 pm
02 • Sacred Contracts

Nikah & Marriage Services

Facilitating official solemnization of Islamic marriage in accordance with traditional jurisprudence, complete with proper certification, pre-marital counseling guidelines, and registry guidance.

📜 Official Certificates Issued Upon Completion
03 • Community Care

Funeral & Janazah Coordination

Standing beside families during moments of bereavement. Offering immediate, comprehensive support for ritual washing (Ghusl), shrouding (Kafn), Janazah prayer, and local burial transport logistics.

📞 24/7 Urgent Bereavement Support Helpline Available
04 • Outreach

Masjid Tours & School Visits

Building bridges of mutual understanding. We warmly welcome local schools, multi-faith forums, and cultural tour societies to experience Islamic art, architecture, and core practices firsthand.

🏛️ Free Guided Bookings for Educational Institutes
